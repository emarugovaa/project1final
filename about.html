~ History of Sweetie ~

Sweetie started in 1965 as the first Sweets Store in the Balkans.We started with one little shop in Prishtina and today we have expanded to 10 shops in Kosovo, 3 in Albania and 1 in Montenegro.

~ Sweeties Secrets ~

We have no secrets considering that we as a company share almost all of our recipes with our costumers.
